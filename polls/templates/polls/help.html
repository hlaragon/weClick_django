weClick is a FREE web-based alternative to a physical clicker devices used for taking polls real-time. Instead of using a physical clicker device you can now use a laptop, tablet or even your smartphone to respond to polls conducted during a class, group meeting, presentation etc. The person conducting the poll (administrator) can control when answers are being received and can display the votes received in an easy to understand color-coded bar-graph. The respondents can select one of the options (A through E) and send the vote anonymously. The administrator has no knowledge of who has cast each particular vote.
Some possible uses for this website are: asking a multiple choice question during a class and collecting their answers to help gage student understnading of the material, conducting an anonymous vote on an issue during a group meeting, getting quick feedback after a presentation.

We hope you will enjoy using weClick!

Tutorial

Administrator Page

  1. Sign up or Sign in is as an administrator
  2. Press the 'Start' button to begin polling
    • The timer will start counting up
    • The Respondent page will now accept answers
  3. Press the 'Stop' button when you want to stop the polling
    • The Respondent page will no longer accept answers
  4. Press the 'Show Results' button to display the responses to the poll
    • The responses will be displayed in the form of a bar graph on the right side of the page
    • The height of each of the bars will be proportionate to the number of responses given for each of the options
  5. Press the 'Start' button again to start a new poll
    • The timer will reset and start counting up from 00:00:00 again
    • The Respondent page will once again start accepting answers

Respondent Page

  1. Sign up or Sign in is as a respondent
  2. Press/Tap on one of the choices
    • The selected choice will be grayed out
    • The ‘Submit’ button will no longer be grayed out and will be active
  3. Press the ‘Submit’ button to send your response
    • The ‘Submit’ button will become grayed out and inactive
    • No more answers can be submitted until a new poll is started
